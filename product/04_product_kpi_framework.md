# Product KPI Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the product KPI framework used in the BESS Product Intelligence Lab V1 prototype.

The goal is to connect technical battery-market outputs with product-management metrics that can support customer value, product decisions, roadmap prioritization, and cross-functional alignment.

---

## KPI Philosophy

A strong BESS software product should not only track technical performance. It should also track whether the product is creating customer value, reducing friction, supporting commercial conversations, and improving user trust.

The V1 KPI framework therefore combines:

1. Customer value KPIs
2. Market and revenue KPIs
3. Grid-constraint KPIs
4. Product usage KPIs
5. Operational KPIs
6. Roadmap prioritization KPIs
7. Strategic product KPIs

---

# 1. Customer Value KPIs

## Revenue Uplift vs Baseline

Measures how much additional value the best strategy creates compared with the Day-Ahead-only baseline.

### Formula

```text
Revenue uplift = Best scenario revenue - Day-Ahead baseline revenue
